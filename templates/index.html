Welcome to Sigmaxm

Cloud platform for management of complex cloud container infrastructure.

What is sigmaxm.io ?

sigmaxm.io is a fully managed docker Swarm and Kubernetes container orchestration and management platform with support to cloud providers Amazon AWS, Google Cloud Platform and Microsoft Azure.

Key Features


Bring your AWS or Azure account

Deploy in your own cloud account, leverage hosting credits and keep full admin access to your machines.

Virtual Private Cloud support

Protect your MongoDB and Redis clusters by hosting in Amazon VPC or Azure VNET public or private subnets.

On Demand Scaling

Dynamically scale up your CPU, memory and disk size IOPS through on demand scaling.

SSL & Disk Encryption

Enable an additional layer of security with SSL based access and encrypt your data and backups at rest.
